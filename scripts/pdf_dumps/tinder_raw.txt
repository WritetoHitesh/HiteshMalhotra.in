This solution works by delivering highly
personalized and relevant date
suggestions through AI-driven matching
and real-time data integration. Seamless
booking and continuous feedback
refinement ensure a user-friendly
experience and ongoing improvement.
Both users are prompted to fill in their
preferences for the date. This includes:
Meeting Place: Users can select or
suggest locations that are convenient for
both.
Dining Preferences: Options for cuisine
types, dietary restrictions, and preferred
dining atmospheres.
Activity Choices: Interests and hobbies,
ranging from outdoor activities to cultural
events.Step 1: Gathering Preferences
The app uses AI to cross-reference the
inputs from both users, identifying
common preferences and highly rated
options in their vicinity. This ensures that
the suggestions are not only convenient
but also aligned with what both users
enjoyStep 2: Intelligent Matching
Based on the matched preferences, the app

distance, user ratings, and available booking
options.Step 3: Real-Time Suggestions
