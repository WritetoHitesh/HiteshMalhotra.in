Augmented Reality (AR) Pickup Points: How it works?
Implement AR pickup points at popular locations like malls, airports, and transit stations. Users use
the Ola app for AR-guided navigation to designated pickup points, helping drivers find them faster.
Community Pickup Spots:
Establish community pickup spots in residential areas for users to walk to, reducing driver travel
