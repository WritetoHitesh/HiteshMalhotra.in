Spotify is a digital music service which allows you access to millions of songs and 
podcasts for free
Spotify operates on a freemium business model. That means that some of its 
services can be accessed for free while others can be accessed by paying a fee
Increase the number of active subscribers to the Spotify Premium plan
In this case, let us define the active subscribers of Spotify Premium as 
those subscribers who open the Spotify app for any 14 days after the 
billing date of this month and before the billing date of next month. 
The amount of time spent on Spotify on each of this 14 days or two 
weeks does not matter.

Active Subscribers of Spotify Premium = % Premium subscribers * 
Subscribers who open the app 14/30 days after the billing date and 
before next billing date

Why 14 days?
Taking an example of Spotify Premium mini challenge, the challenge 
encourages you to listen to music for 14 day
