## Strategic Analysis

**Core Problem Space:**
No office canteen service - explicit Swiggy food searching is tiring.

**Primary Solution Pattern:**
Introduce a new feature to get regular and healthy cuisine lunch meals easily without explicit searching & ordering, and manageable price

**Target Outcome Metric:**
Daily users looking for HMM (Home Made Meals)
