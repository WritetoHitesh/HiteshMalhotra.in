# The Vision

At Learnify Technologies, we set out to build **Aavtaar.ai**, a flagship AI venture focused on creating conversational AI voice platforms. As the AI Product Manager, my role was to define the platform's vision, create the roadmap, and drive execution with a cross-functional engineering team of 20 members.

## Establishing the Product Foundation

To transition from ideation to delivery, I took ownership of the core product artifacts:
*   [✓] Authored comprehensive **Product Requirement Documents (PRDs)** that bridged business requirements with technical constraints involving Large Language Models (LLMs).
*   [✓] Broke down complex AI interactions into clear **user stories** and defined strict acceptance criteria.
*   [✓] Established an ongoing **release planning** cycle to ensure predictable delivery to stakeholders.

## Agile Execution

Building an AI voice product requires tight feedback loops. We fully integrated Agile practices into our workflow:
*   Led rigorous sprint planning sessions to prioritize features based on user value.
*   Conducted regular backlog grooming to ensure engineering was always unblocked and aligned with the strategic roadmap.

## Key Outcomes
By aligning our 20-person team under a unified product vision and agile methodology, we successfully launched the core AI voice platform, paving the way for scalable Conversational AI solutions.
