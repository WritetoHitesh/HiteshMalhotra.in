# Truecaller Product Teardown

## Executive Summary
The document analyzes Truecaller's business models, target audience, and product features, including its mission, competitors, and user personas.

## Strategic Analysis

**Core Problem Space:**
Excessive interruption from unknown and low-priority calls, risk of missing important calls by ignoring all unknown numbers

**Primary Solution Pattern:**
Smart call filtering system to prioritize calls from Investors and clients & block unwanted sales and spam calls

**Target Outcome Metric:**
User engagement and satisfaction with the app's ability to filter out unwanted calls
